About

MiLOLnaire is a game inspired by a popular TV show. Your goal is to correctly answer 12 questions to become the MiLOLnaire! But be careful—the questions get progressively harder. Don't worry, though!
You have 4 lifelines at your disposal:
  • Fifty-fifty: delete two wrong answers.
  • Call your friend: get help from friend You chose.
  • Ask the audience: get percentages for each answer, based on audience.
  • Refresh question: don't like current question? Get a new one!
